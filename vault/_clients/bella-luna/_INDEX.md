# Bella Luna Toys -- Client Workspace

**Account:** act_551906995157520 (Meta Ads)
**Category:** Waldorf-style wooden toys and natural play products
**Strategy:** Momentum-based scaling with a clean 2-campaign structure

## Account Overview

Bella Luna Toys runs Meta Ads through a consolidated 2-campaign structure: a primary CBO campaign (Consolidated Primary Sales -- Adv+ CBO) at $350/day and a seasonal ABO campaign (Seasonal Sales -- Adv+ ABO). The account previously had 70+ campaigns before consolidation in late 2024. The strategic approach is momentum-based -- when performance is strong, scale aggressively; when it dips, pull back and rotate creative.

## Performance Trajectory

### The Consolidation Story (Late 2024)
The account hit its lowest point in Sep-Oct 2024 (6-8x ROAS, $22 CPA) while running 70+ fragmented campaigns. The decision to consolidate down to 2 campaigns in November 2024 was the foundational shift. ROAS immediately recovered to 11x in Nov-Dec 2024.

### The Patient Year (2025)
2025 was a year of patience. Q1 saw a deliberate pullback ($840-$1,221/month). Mid-year softened (7-8x ROAS in Apr-Jun). Recovery began in July and built through October. Then the Q4 scale-up proved the structure could handle 4-5x normal spend: $19,298 combined Nov-Dec spend at 13.2x ROAS with 1,503 purchases.

### The Breakout (Q1 2026)
Instead of the usual post-holiday pullback, spend was maintained at $5k-$10k/month. The results shattered every previous benchmark. February 2026 hit 21.76x ROAS with 1,237 purchases on $10k spend -- the best month in account history. Q1 2026 has already generated more revenue ($419,699) than all of 2025 ($310,774).
