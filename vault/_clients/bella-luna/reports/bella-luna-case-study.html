The Story

From fragmented to elite.

Before Voltage

Bella Luna's Meta Ads account was running 70+ fragmented campaigns with inconsistent structure and no unified strategy. While the product resonated with audiences (ROAS occasionally spiked above 15x), performance was volatile. The account hit its lowest point in September-October 2024 at 6-8x ROAS with CPAs above $20. Monthly revenue averaged $30,968 with no clear scaling path.

After Voltage

Voltage implemented a three-phase transformation. Consolidation from 70+ campaigns to 2. Momentum-based scaling with gradient budget adjustments tied to ROAS. Disciplined creative rotation with UGC content. Average monthly revenue increased from $30,968 to $76,922 while blended ROAS improved from 11.0x to 15.0x.

Current: March 2026 (18 days)

18.18x ROAS  |  $135,316 Revenue  |  891 Purchases  |  $8.35 CPA

Q1 2026 has already surpassed all of 2025's total revenue ($419K vs $311K)
