What We Did

Four strategic shifts.

Every change was deliberate, data-driven, and designed to compound over time.

Looking Ahead

What comes next.

01

Maintain Current Spend Posture

At 18x+ ROAS, the momentum-based strategy says keep pushing. No reason to pull back until performance signals otherwise.

02

Creative Refresh Pipeline

Frequency at 2.82 approaching the 3.0x cap. CTR decline signals creative fatigue. UGC production should be ongoing, not reactive.

03

Monitor CPC/CTR Divergence

CPC rising 48% ($0.44 to $0.65) while CTR drops 51% (3.89% to 1.91%). The algorithm is compensating with better conversions, but this has limits.

04

Q2 Seasonal Planning

Historical data shows mid-year softness (7-8x in 2025 Q2). The consolidated structure should handle it better, but creative rotation will be critical.

05

Explore AI Video Creative

AI-rendered video ads represent the next frontier for scaling creative production without bottlenecks. Currently exploring with Kling for sister accounts.
