## Key Observations

### Q1-Q2: Strong Baseline (Mar-Aug)
- Consistent 11x-17x ROAS across 6 months
- Spend held steady at $2,000-$2,200/month after the March ramp
- August peaked at 17.50x ROAS -- the best efficiency of the year
- CPA stayed under $14 for most of this period

### Sep-Oct Seasonal Trough
- ROAS dropped sharply to 7.67x in September and 6.17x in October
- CPA spiked to $17.49 and $22.25 respectively
- This is the classic back-to-school/pre-holiday dead zone for gift-oriented e-commerce
- October spend increased to $3,760 (likely early holiday testing) but efficiency lagged

### Nov-Dec Holiday Surge
- November: $5,925 spend, 397 purchases, 10.99x ROAS
- December: $4,954 spend, 340 purchases, 11.00x ROAS
- Combined holiday season: $10,879 spend, 737 purchases, ~11.0x ROAS
- Holiday purchasing intent brought ROAS back above 10x despite 2-3x higher spend
- CTR climbed to 4.33-4.66%, the highest of the year

## Notes

This was the year the account transitioned from a fragmented multi-campaign structure to a consolidated 2-campaign approach. The consolidation decision was made around November 2024. See [[decisions/2024-retrospective-consolidation]] for details.
