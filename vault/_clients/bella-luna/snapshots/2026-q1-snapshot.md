Q1 2026 represents the best sustained performance in the account's history. In under 3 months, the account has already generated more revenue ($419,699) than all of 2025 ($310,774).

### February 2026 -- Best Month Ever
- 21.76x ROAS on $10,055 spend -- the highest ROAS at this spend level in the entire dataset

- $8.13 CPA -- among the lowest ever recorded

### March 2026 -- On Pace to Beat February
- 18 days in: $7,442 spend, $135,316 revenue, 891 purchases

- CPC has been climbing: $0.44 (Jan) to $0.52 (Feb) to $0.65 (Mar)

- Despite these efficiency headwinds, conversion rates and AOV are clearly compensating
- The algorithm is finding high-value buyers even as top-of-funnel metrics shift
