## The Bella Luna Problem

HABA and Bella Luna sell the same product category (wooden toys) under the same parent company. Bella Luna runs at 13.4x blended ROAS. HABA runs at 5.53x. Same media buyer, same strategy framework, dramatically different results. The gap is driven by:
- Brand positioning (Bella Luna has clearer US market fit)
- Creative assets (Bella Luna is more UGC-friendly)
- Higher CPCs on HABA (structural cost disadvantage)
- Lower conversion rates on HABA's traffic
