# HABA USA -- Client Workspace

## Account Overview

HABA is a German wooden toy manufacturer and the parent company of Bella Luna Toys. The Meta ad account (act_941543396233578) has been actively managed since March 2024. HABA sells premium wooden toys, games, and children's products -- the same product category as Bella Luna but under a distinctly German brand identity.

HABA struggles with American market positioning. The brand lacks the whimsical, UGC-friendly aesthetic that makes Bella Luna's creative work so well on Meta. CPCs run higher, conversion rates run lower, and the account is heavily dependent on Q4 holiday demand to hit acceptable annual numbers.

**25-Month Totals (Mar 2024 -- Mar 2026):** $34,589 spend | $191,231 revenue | 2,345 purchases | 5.53x blended ROAS

## The Bella Luna Comparison Problem

HABA and Bella Luna sell the same product category under the same parent company, managed by the same media buyer with the same strategic framework. The performance gap is stark:

| Metric | HABA | Bella Luna |
|--------|------|------------|
| Blended ROAS | 5.53x | 13.4x |
| Off-season ROAS | 3-4x | 8-10x |
| CPC | $0.50-$1.27 | Lower |
| Creative style | German/institutional | UGC-friendly/whimsical |

HABA runs at roughly one-third of Bella Luna's efficiency on similar products. The gap is not a media buying problem -- it is a brand positioning and creative problem. Bella Luna's creative resonates with American parents on Meta; HABA's does not, at least not consistently.

## Performance Trajectory

HABA's performance is inconsistent and holiday-dependent:

- **2024 (10 months):** ~$14,932 spend, ~$90,230 revenue, ~798 purchases, ~6.04x blended ROAS. May spike to 8.03x. Q4 strong (Nov 6.81x, Dec 6.23x).
- **2025 (12 months):** ~$13,847 spend, ~$84,827 revenue, ~845 purchases, ~6.13x blended ROAS. Brutal Q1-Q2 (Feb 2.63x, May 2.23x). Q4 breakout (Nov 9.72x -- best month ever, Dec 7.61x).
- **2026 Q1 (through Mar 18):** $5,753 spend, $24,013 revenue, 500 purchases, 4.17x blended ROAS. Inconsistent month-to-month but volume and CPA are trending better YoY.

The pattern: Q4 holidays produce 7-10x ROAS, the other 9 months hover around 3-4x. The annual blended number (5.53x) is propped up by two months of holiday demand.

## Current State

**Budget:** $60/day on the Consolidated Evergreen Advantage+ campaign
**Active campaigns:** 3 (Consolidated Evergreen, Seasonal ABO, Amazon LPV traffic -- last excluded from analysis)
**Paused campaigns:** 40+ legacy campaigns from Madgicx/1o8 eras
**March 2026 MTD:** 4.02x ROAS, $16.04 CPA, $0.21 CPC, 7.09% CTR

The account is at the healthy threshold but not scaling. The $60/day budget caps growth potential, and there is limited confidence that increasing spend outside of Q4 would maintain efficiency.
