# TruNiagen Performance Benchmarks

**Product:** NAD+ supplement (ChromaDex brand TruNiagen)
**Strategy model:** Conservative LTV-based. Decisions are measured against lifetime customer value, not immediate ROAS.

## ROAS
**ROAS threshold:** 1.5x (adjusted for lifetime value -- appears low but accounts for subscription revenue and repeat purchases)

## Campaign Structure
Mix of ABO and CBO campaigns

## Key Metrics Tracked
- Purchases
- Purchase conversion value
- Outbound clicks
- Outbound click rate
- Outbound CPA
- Purchase conversion ROAS
- Spend
- Custom conversion: Subscriptions (unique to TruNiagen -- other clients do not have this)

## LTV Adjustment
TruNiagen is the only client with an LTV adjustment ruleset. When evaluating performance, raw ROAS understates actual value because it doesn't capture subscription lifetime revenue. Apply the LTV multiplier when comparing against benchmarks.
