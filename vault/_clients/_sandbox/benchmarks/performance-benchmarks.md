# Ember & Bloom Performance Benchmarks

**Product:** Premium DTC coffee subscription
**Strategy model:** Balanced growth. Subscription conversion is the primary KPI.

## Meta Ads Benchmarks
- **ROAS threshold:** 4.0x blended
- **CPA target:** $28.00 (subscription), $18.00 (one-time purchase)
- **CTR target:** 1.8%+
- **CPM target:** $12.00-$18.00
- **Frequency cap:** 3x per 7 days (prospecting), 6x per 7 days (retargeting)
- **Acceptable CPA range:** $22.00-$34.00 (outside this = anomaly)
- **Acceptable ROAS range:** 3.2x-6.0x (outside this = anomaly)

## Google Ads Benchmarks
- **ROAS threshold:** 5.0x (search), 3.0x (shopping)
- **CPA target:** $22.00 (search), $26.00 (shopping)
- **CTR target:** 4.5%+ (search), 1.2%+ (shopping)

## Key Metrics Tracked
- Purchases (total and subscription vs one-time)
- Purchase conversion value
- Subscription conversion rate
- Outbound clicks and CTR
- CPA (subscription and one-time)
- ROAS (blended and by campaign)
- Spend (daily and weekly)
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC)
