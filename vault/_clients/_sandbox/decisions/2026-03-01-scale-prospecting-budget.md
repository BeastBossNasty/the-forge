# Scale prospecting budget 20% on Meta

**Decision:** Increase daily prospecting budget from $150/day to $180/day on the Consolidated Primary CBO campaign.

**Reasoning:**
CPA has been steady at $25 for the past two weeks, well within the $22-$34 acceptable range. ROAS is at 4.8x, above the 4.0x threshold. The [[meta-ads-playbook]] recommends scaling in 10-20% increments. This is a 20% increase, at the upper end of the recommended range.

**Expected outcome:**
CPA may spike briefly (48h) but should settle back to $26-$28 range within 4 days. Weekly spend increases from ~$1,050 to ~$1,260. If CPA stays under $30 and ROAS stays above 3.5x, the scale is working.

**Review date:** 2026-03-08
