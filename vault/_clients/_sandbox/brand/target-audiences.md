# Ember & Bloom Target Audiences

## Primary: The Mindful Coffee Lover
**Demographics:** Age 28-45, HHI $75k+, urban/suburban
**Interests:** Specialty coffee, cooking, sustainability, wellness
**Pain points:** Tired of mediocre grocery store coffee. Wants quality but doesn't want to be a "coffee snob." Hates running out of coffee.
**Buying triggers:** Taste discovery (new origins), convenience (subscription), gifting seasons

## Secondary: The Gift Giver
**Demographics:** Age 30-55, HHI $60k+
**Interests:** Thoughtful gifting, food & drink, holiday shopping
**Pain points:** Wants a gift that feels personal and premium without being generic. Hard to find gifts for "the person who has everything."
**Buying triggers:** Holiday seasons, birthdays, corporate gifting, "just because" occasions
