# Ember & Bloom Creative Guidelines

## Visual Style
- Warm tones: amber, deep brown, cream, forest green
- Natural lighting preferred over studio
- Show the coffee in context (morning routine, kitchen, hands wrapped around a mug)
- Lifestyle shots outperform product-only shots 2:1

## Ad Formats
- Instagram/Facebook: 1080x1080 (feed), 1080x1920 (stories/reels)
- Video: 15-30 seconds, hook in first 3 seconds
- UGC-style outperforms polished brand content

## Creative Rotation Rules
- Minimum 3 active creatives per ad set
- Replace when CTR drops 30%+ from peak or frequency exceeds 3x
- Seasonal rotation: spring/summer (iced coffee, cold brew), fall/winter (cozy, warm, gift sets)

## Performance Triggers
- CTR below 1.2%: refresh creative
- Frequency above 3x in prospecting: add new creative or expand audience
- CPA above $34: check creative fatigue first before adjusting targeting
