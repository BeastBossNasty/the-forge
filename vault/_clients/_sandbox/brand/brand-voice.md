# Ember & Bloom Brand Voice

## Tone
Warm, knowledgeable, unpretentious. Like a friend who happens to know a lot about coffee and wants to share the good stuff with you. Never snobby. Never preachy about sustainability (let the actions speak).

## Key Messages
- "Coffee worth waking up for" (tagline)
- Direct trade means better coffee and better lives for farmers
- Small-batch roasting preserves what makes each origin unique
- Subscription means never running out and always getting peak freshness

## Do
- Use sensory language (aroma, flavor notes, warmth)
- Be specific about origins and processes
- Keep it conversational and approachable
- Mention the farmers and their stories when relevant

## Don't
- Use coffee jargon without explanation (no "cupping scores" without context)
- Sound elitist or gatekeeping
- Trash other coffee brands
- Overuse sustainability buzzwords
