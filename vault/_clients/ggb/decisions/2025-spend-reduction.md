# Decision: Reduce Annual Spend and Focus on Seasonal Peaks

## Context

After a turbulent 2024 that saw the GGB Meta Ads account spend $496,714 at only 2.11x blended ROAS -- including two catastrophic months (Aug-Sep at 0.74x and 0.90x) and an overscaled December ($160k spend at 1.90x) -- a fundamental strategy shift was needed.

## The Decision

Reduce annual Meta Ads spend from ~$497,000 to ~$101,000 (an 80% reduction) by:

1. **Eliminating off-season overspend:** Keep non-peak months at $2,000-$10,000 instead of $20,000-$60,000
2. **Concentrating budget on seasonal peaks:** Allocate the bulk of spend to Mother's Day (May), the holiday season (Nov-Dec), and other gifting occasions
3. **Right-sizing December:** Reduce December spend from $160,000 to $20,000, prioritizing efficiency over volume
4. **Accepting lower total volume:** Fewer total purchases is acceptable if the account stops losing money in dead months

## Reasoning

- The 2024 Aug-Sep crisis proved that $20k/month in non-gifting months produces negative returns
- December 2024 showed diminishing returns at extreme scale: 1.90x ROAS on $160k is barely profitable
- July 2024 (3.66x on $20k) and May 2024 (2.38x on $59k) showed that seasonal demand drives efficiency
- The account's best months are always tied to gifting occasions, not to spend levels

## Expected Outcome

- Higher blended ROAS (targeting 2.5x+ vs. 2024's 2.11x)
- Elimination of money-losing months
- Better December efficiency at right-sized spend
- Lower total revenue but better return on every dollar spent

## Actual Outcome

The strategy was validated across multiple dimensions:

### Blended ROAS Improvement
- 2024: 2.11x on $496,714 spend
- 2025: 2.72x on $100,840 spend -- a 29% ROAS improvement

### Seasonal Peak Efficiency
- **May 2025:** 7.74x ROAS on $4,507 (vs. May 2024: 2.38x on $59,296) -- Mother's Day was the standout
- **Jul 2025:** 6.77x ROAS on $2,103 (vs. Jul 2024: 3.66x on $20,288)
- **Nov 2025:** 6.63x ROAS on $3,762 (vs. Nov 2024: 1.05x on $28,254)
- **Dec 2025:** 5.62x ROAS on $20,187 (vs. Dec 2024: 1.90x on $160,015) -- the biggest single improvement

### Off-Season Damage Control
- Aug 2025: 3.73x on $7,781 (vs. Aug 2024: 0.74x on $19,774) -- from losing money to profitable
- Sep 2025: 0.92x on $3,932 (vs. Sep 2024: 0.90x on $20,736) -- still weak, but $17,000 less wasted

### Remaining Weakness
- Q1 2025 was still brutal: Jan 0.99x, Feb 0.58x, Mar 0.70x
- Sep-Oct 2025 remained below breakeven (0.92x and 1.19x)
- These months may need near-zero spend rather than $3,000-$10,000

## 4D Score

**Rating: Improved**

The 80% spend reduction was the correct strategic response to the 2024 performance data. Every seasonal peak performed better at lower spend, and the catastrophic off-season losses were largely eliminated. December 2025 alone validates the decision -- achieving 5.62x ROAS on $20k spend versus 1.90x on $160k is a dramatic efficiency gain. The remaining optimization opportunity is to reduce Q1 and Sep-Oct spend even further, potentially to near-zero maintenance levels.
