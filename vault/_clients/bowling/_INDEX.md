## The Big Picture

Bowling.com is one of the most consistently high-performing Meta ad accounts you will ever see. Over 25 months (March 2024 -- March 2026), this single-campaign account has generated:

- **$6,037,018 in revenue** on **$348,747 in spend**
- **43,760 purchases** at a **17.31x blended ROAS**
- CPCs as low as **$0.07** (2024) and currently at **$0.09-$0.10** (2026)
- CTR climbing from ~3-5% in 2024 to **7.5-8.0%** in 2026

The account runs a single campaign with a single "Broad Match" ad set. No complex structure. No elaborate funnel. Just one campaign doing exactly what it should.

## Account History

### 2024: The Growth Year (~$184,706 spend, ~$2,914,704 revenue, ~15.78x ROAS)

The account ramped up through 2024. The critical event was the **June 2024 overspend** -- scaling to $29.5k/month blew CPA to $15.20 and cratered ROAS to 8.56x. This was the only time the account dropped below 12x. The lesson: this account has a hard ceiling on efficient spend, and scaling too fast kills CPA.

The highlight was **December 2024** -- 28.66x ROAS, $610,223 revenue on $21,291 spend. The single best month in the account's history, driven by holiday demand.

### 2025: The Efficiency Pivot (~$138,414 spend, ~$2,998,765 revenue, ~21.67x ROAS)

The June 2024 lesson was applied. Total spend was cut 25% (from ~$185k to ~$138k), but revenue actually grew 2.9%. Blended ROAS improved 37%, from 15.78x to 21.67x. CPCs dropped from ~$0.19 to ~$0.12. Only October 2025 (11.08x) dipped below 15x ROAS.

2025 proved that spending less -- and spending smarter -- produces better results for this account.

### 2026 Q1: On Pace for the Best Year Yet ($25,631 spend, $458,420 revenue, 17.88x ROAS)

Through March 18, 2026, the account continues to perform. CPCs at all-time lows ($0.09-$0.10), CTR at all-time highs (7.5-8.0%), CPA within target across all months.

## Current State (March 18, 2026)

- **March MTD:** 16.83x ROAS, $8.91 CPA, $7,521 spend, $126,549 revenue
- **Structure:** Single campaign, single Broad Match ad set
- **Creative initiative:** Currently building AI-rendered video ads with **Kling** for the next creative phase
- **Strategy:** Efficiency-first. No aggressive scaling planned.

## Key Decisions

1. **June 2024 Overspend Lesson** -- Do not scale above ~$20k/month without extreme caution. The single-campaign structure does not absorb aggressive budget increases.
2. **2025 Efficiency Focus** -- Prioritize ROAS over volume. Spending less produced more revenue.
