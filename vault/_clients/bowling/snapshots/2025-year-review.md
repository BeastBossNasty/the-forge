## Key Observations

**The efficiency pivot worked.** Pulled back total spend from ~$185k to ~$138k (25% reduction) while revenue actually increased slightly ($2.91M to $2.99M). Blended ROAS improved from 15.78x to 21.67x -- a 37% improvement in return per dollar spent.

**CPC compression:** CPCs dropped into the $0.11-$0.14 range all year (down from $0.20+ in 2024). CTR climbed steadily from 3.99% in January to 7.92% in November, indicating improving creative performance and audience targeting.

**Only one dip below 15x:** October 2025 at 11.08x ROAS ($12.59 CPA). This was the weakest month of the year but still far better than the June 2024 disaster (8.56x). The account maintained discipline.

**Q4 2025 vs Q4 2024:** Q4 2025 delivered $548,089 revenue on $36,368 spend (15.07x). Q4 2024 delivered $1,356,000 revenue on $69,488 spend (19.51x). Lower absolute revenue in Q4 2025, but spend was also cut significantly. The account did not try to chase the Dec 2024 moonshot.

**Best month:** August 2025 -- 22.87x ROAS, $255,365 revenue, strong summer performance with only $11,164 spend.

**Lesson applied:** The June 2024 overspend lesson was internalized. 2025 never pushed a single month above $15k in spend (except Sep at $14.7k), keeping CPA firmly within the $10 target range for most of the year.
