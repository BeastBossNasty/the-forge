# June 2024 Overspend -- Scaling Limits Lesson

## What Happened

In May-June 2024, the Bowling.com Meta account was aggressively scaled from ~$7k/month (April) to $19.8k (May) and then $29.6k (June). This was a 4x increase in spend over two months.

## The Numbers

| Month | Spend | ROAS | CPA | CPC |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Apr 2024 | $6,754 | 16.44x | $7.99 | $0.10 |
| May 2024 | $19,842 | 12.01x | $11.22 | $0.21 |
| **Jun 2024** | **$29,552** | **8.56x** | **$15.20** | **$0.28** |
| Jul 2024 | $16,304 | 14.88x | $9.21 | $0.28 |
| Aug 2024 | $18,468 | 16.00x | $8.37 | $0.26 |

## What Went Wrong

- **CPA blew past target:** $15.20 CPA vs the $10 target -- a 52% overshoot
- **ROAS cratered to 8.56x:** Well below the 12x caution threshold, and the worst month in the account's entire 25-month history
- **CPC doubled:** From $0.10 in April to $0.28 in June, indicating the algorithm was reaching into expensive, lower-quality audience segments to spend the budget
- **CTR dropped:** From 5.32% to 3.12%, confirming audience quality degradation

## The Recovery

Spend was pulled back to $16,304 in July. ROAS recovered to 14.88x. By August ($18,468 spend, 16.00x ROAS), the account was back to healthy performance. The recovery took approximately 6-8 weeks.

## Decision

**Do not scale this account above ~$20k/month without extreme caution.** The single-campaign, single-ad-set structure does not absorb aggressive budget increases well. The algorithm needs time to find efficient audiences at higher spend levels.

## Rules Derived

1. Monthly spend increases should not exceed 20-30% month-over-month
2. If CPA exceeds $12 (120% of target), consider pulling back immediately
3. If ROAS drops below 12x, treat it as a caution threshold
4. This account performs best in the $8k-$15k/month spend range with occasional seasonal pushes to $20k-$25k

## 4D Score: Worsened

This was a negative outcome that produced a critical learning. The aggressive scaling damaged short-term performance but the lesson directly informed the 2025 efficiency-first strategy that produced superior results.
