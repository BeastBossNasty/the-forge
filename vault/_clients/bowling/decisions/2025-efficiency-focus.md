# 2025 Efficiency Focus -- Prioritize ROAS Over Volume

## Decision

Starting in 2025, shift the Bowling.com Meta account strategy from volume-driven scaling to efficiency-first management. Reduce total annual spend while maintaining or growing revenue through tighter CPA discipline.

## Rationale

The June 2024 overspend (see [decisions/2024-jun-overspend-lesson.md](2024-jun-overspend-lesson.md)) proved that this account has a hard ceiling on efficient spend. Pushing beyond ~$20k/month degrades CPA rapidly. Rather than chasing raw revenue volume, the strategy should focus on maximizing return per dollar spent.

## Results

| Metric | 2024 | 2025 | Change |
|---|---|---|---|
| Total Spend | ~$184,706 | ~$138,414 | -25% |
| Total Revenue | ~$2,914,704 | ~$2,998,765 | +2.9% |
| Total Purchases | ~21,606 | ~20,156 | -6.7% |
| Blended ROAS | ~15.78x | ~21.67x | +37% |
| Avg CPC | ~$0.19 | ~$0.12 | -37% |

## What This Proves

- **Spending less produced more revenue.** A 25% reduction in spend yielded a 2.9% increase in revenue.
- **ROAS improved by 37%.** From 15.78x to 21.67x blended across the full year.
- **CPC dropped 37%.** The algorithm found cheaper, higher-quality traffic when not forced to spend aggressively.
- **CTR nearly doubled over the year.** From ~4% in early 2025 to ~8% by late 2025, indicating improving audience match and creative quality.
- **Only one month below 15x ROAS.** October 2025 at 11.08x was the only dip, compared to multiple sub-15x months in 2024.

## Ongoing Application

This efficiency-first approach continues into 2026. Monthly spend is being held in the $7k-$10k range with no plans for aggressive scaling. The account is performing at all-time low CPCs ($0.09-$0.10) and all-time high CTRs (7.5-8.0%).

## 4D Score: Improved

Clear positive outcome. The decision to prioritize efficiency over volume produced better results across every meaningful metric.
