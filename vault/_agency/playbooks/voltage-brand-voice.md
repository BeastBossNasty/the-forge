# Voltage Media Brand Voice

## Rules
- No em dashes -- ever, anywhere, for any reason
- Plain English over jargon
- Confident and direct, not hedging or disclaimering
- Boutique expertise + technology adoption positioning

## Identity
- Boutique performance marketing agency, 20+ years operating
- Google Premier Partner (Top 3%)
- Meta Business Partner
- Microsoft Advertising Partner
- Business Portfolio ID: 622383571242852

## Positioning
Core positioning: boutique expertise combined with technology adoption ahead of competitors. We are small enough to care, experienced enough to deliver, and tech-forward enough to outperform agencies 10x our size.

## Writing Style
- Short sentences. Direct statements.
- Lead with the insight, not the setup.
- Use numbers and specifics over vague claims.
- Never use em dashes. Use commas, periods, or parentheses instead.
