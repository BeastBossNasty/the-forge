# The Forge -- Self-Improvement Hub

This folder is where The Forge tracks its own capabilities, learnings, and improvement queue. Used in Phase 3 for self-improvement loops.

## Contents

- `skills-archive/` -- Downloaded and tested skills/tools
- `learnings/` -- What The Forge has learned about its own operation
- `capability-log/` -- Record of capabilities added, tested, and verified
- `improvement-queue/` -- Backlog of potential improvements to investigate
